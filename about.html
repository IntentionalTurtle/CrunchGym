King of the Plates

The biggest and best local competition for deadlift, squats and bench. There are both weigh in category competitions as well as PB milestones for members with great prizes. Earn your place on the wall as the king (or queen) of the plates!

Womens' Workout

No men allowed! The female trainers are available and will assist with beginning or continuing a training program focused on your personal fitness goals. MMA and boxing arenas will also be female-only and reservations can be made for the time slots and sparring partners.

Bodybuilder Pro Seminar

Join local heavyweights Sadie Bucharest and Rob Schieffer as they teach and show what you need to do to take your career in fitness and bodybuilding to the next level. Rob is a former bodybuilder turned MMA coach and Sadie is still currently on the female bodybuilding circuit. Learn what it takes to get into the business of being a fitness and competition professional.

Thirsty Thursday

Every Thursday our staff will have our rotating series of protein and fitness blends at the canteen for half price! Get your pre-workout and post-workout shakes for the day and feel good while you work hard.

Member's Meal

The Elite members look forward to this! Crunch! master nutritionist Sheri Baker cooks a bodybuilder size meal fit for the top of the game! Her bi-weekly meals are protein rich and balanced for maximum support for your needs. On top of that, she even gives the recipes! A perfect perk for our Elite tier members.
